📌 Description

Dragon Tiger is a fast-paced online casino card game developed with Unity for mobile platforms. The game features realtime multiplayer gameplay, smooth UI/UX, responsive touch controls, and optimized performance for Android and iOS devices. Players place bets on either the Dragon or Tiger side, and the highest card wins the round. The gameplay is simple, quick, and designed for casual casino audiences.
